Join():     # Tested: no bugs.
    1. Caller is not connected to vc, signal message.
    2. Caller is connected to vc:
        * Bot is not connected to vc, connect.
        * Bot is connected to vc (different channel, by itself), disconnect bot, connect to current channel.
        * Bot is connected to vc, (different channel, not by itself), signal message.
        * Bot is connected to vc (same channel as caller), signal message.


Leave():    # Tested: no bugs.
    1. Caller is not connected to vc:
        * Bot is not connected to vc, signal message.
        * Bot is connected to vc (by itself), disconnect bot.
        * Bot is connected to vc (not by itself), signal message.
    2. Caller is connected to vc:
        * Bot is not connected to vc, signal message.
        * Bot is connected to vc (by itself), disconnect bot.
        * Bot is connected to vc (same channel as caller), disconnect bot.
        * Bot is connected to vc (not by itself, different channel from caller), signal message.

Play():
    1. Caller is not connected to vc, signal message.
    2. Caller is connected to vc:
        * Bot is not connected to vc, connected bot & play.
        * Bot is connected to vc (different channel, by itself), disconnect bot, connect to current channel, & play.
        * Bot is connected to vc (different channel from caller, not by itself), signale message.

Pause():
    1. Caller is not connected to vc:
        * Bot is not connected to vc, signal message.
        * Bot is connected to vc, signal message.
    2. Caller is connected to vc:
        * Bot is not connected to vc, signal message.
        * Bot is connected to vc (different channel), signal message.
        * Bot is connected to vc (same channel):
            - Bot is playing music, pause the music.
            - Bot is not playing music, signal message.

Resume():
    1. Caller is not connected to vc:
        * Bot is not connected to vc, signal message.
        * Bot is connected to vc, signal message.
    2. Caller is connected to vc:
        * Bot is not connected to vc, signal message.
        * Bot is connected to vc (different channel), signal message.
        * Bot is connected to vc (same channel as caller), resume music.

Skip():
    1. Caller is not connected to vc, signal message.
    2. Caller is connected to vc:
        * Bot is not connected to vc, signal message.
        * Bot is connected to vc:
            - (different channel), signal message.
            - (same channel):
                + Song list is empty, signal message.
                + Song list is not empty, skip song.

List():
    1. Bot is not connected to vc, signal message.
    2. Bot is connected to vc:
        * Song list is empty, signal message.
        * Song list is not empty, show list.
